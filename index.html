-THE IMPORTANCE OF WATER IN OUR LIFE-

What is the importance of water in human life?

Water carries nutrients to all cells in our body and oxygen to our brain.

Water allows the body to absorb and assimilate minerals, vitamins, amino acids, glucose and other substances.

Water flushes out toxins and waste. Water helps to regulate body temperature.

The benefits of drinking water for the human body are:

    - maximize physical performance

    - Significantly affects energy levels and brain function

    - May help prevent and treat headaches.

    - Drinking water giving you healthier skin.
